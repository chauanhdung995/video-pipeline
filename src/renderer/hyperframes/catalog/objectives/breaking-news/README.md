# Breaking News

Tin nóng / Bản tin khẩn

Status: ready

Templates:
- `news-alert-opener`: Mau mo dau tin nong: alert badge, headline lon, subhead va metadata cap nhat.
- `issue-comparison`: Mau so sanh hai van de, hai phien ban, hoac hai kich ban trong mot tin nong.
- `news-bullet-list`: Mau list danh sach toi da 5 muc cho cac diem can biet trong breaking news.
- `event-timeline`: Mau timeline thoi gian su kien xay ra, toi da 5 moc.
- `quote-card`: Mau trich dan loi noi cua nhan vat, nguon tin, hoac co quan lien quan.
- `visual-evidence`: Mau trich bang chung hinh anh: anh hien truong, screenshot, tai lieu, bieu do hoac khung hinh lam bang chung.
- `key-number`: Mau lam noi bat mot con so, su kien hoac metric quan trong trong ban tin.
- `follow-outro`: Mau ket thuc keu goi follow/subscribe kenh de nhan cap nhat breaking news tiep theo.
- `data-snapshot-chart`: Mau bieu do so lieu cho breaking news: cot + duong + metric chinh.
- `source-check`: Mau phan biet thong tin da xac nhan va thong tin can than trong, rat can cho breaking news.
- `live-update-ticker`: Mau live update/ticker cho cac cap nhat ngan trong luc tin dang dien ra.
- `location-context`: Mau dia diem/ban do de dat su kien vao boi canh khong gian.
